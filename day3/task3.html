Introduction

Earth is the third planet from the Sun, the only known celestial body to support life. It's characterized by its diverse ecosystems, including oceans, mountains, and forests, and its unique features like liquid water and a stable atmosphere. Earth is a vital home for all living things, providing the resources and conditions necessary for survival.

Basic Facts About Earth
